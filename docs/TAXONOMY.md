# IntentMirror Behavioral Attack Surface Taxonomy

IntentMirror classifies manipulation tactics into practical, user-facing vectors.

## Core vectors

1. **Panic Triggering**  
   Fear-heavy urgency intended to collapse reflection time.

2. **Authority Impersonation**  
   Message/interface claims institutional authority to force obedience.

3. **Scarcity Engineering**  
   Limited-time/limited-stock framing to induce impulse decisions.

4. **Forced Consent**  
   Interface structure nudges acceptance while hiding decline paths.

5. **Trust Laundering**  
   Borrowed trust signals (badges, pseudo-compliance language) without reliable proof.

6. **Confusion Architecture**  
   Layout and wording designed to increase cognitive load and wrong-click probability.

## Scoring philosophy

- Score represents **decision manipulation risk**, not malware certainty.
- IntentMirror prefers explainability over opaque confidence metrics.
- High score should always include:
  - observed cues,
  - likely tactic,
  - recommended verification step.
